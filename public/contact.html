Frequently Asked Questions

How can I enroll my child in the academy?

To enroll your child, you can complete our online registration form or visit our admissions office during office hours. We'll schedule a skill assessment to determine the appropriate training group for your child.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept players from ages 6 to 19, divided into appropriate age groups: U8, U10, U13, U15, U17, and U19.

What are the training schedules?

Training schedules vary by age group. Generally, younger groups train 2-3 times per week, while older groups train 3-5 times per week. Detailed schedules are provided upon enrollment.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer a limited number of scholarships and financial aid packages based on both financial need and player potential. Please contact our admissions office for more information.

What qualifications do your coaches have?

All our coaches hold at minimum a UEFA B License or equivalent, with many holding UEFA A or Pro Licenses. Many are former professional players with extensive playing and coaching experience.
